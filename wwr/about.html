Welcome to Wireframe Rafting

Wireframe Rafting

Wireframe Rafting is a family-owned business that has been providing whitewater rafting adventures for over 25 years. We are dedicated to providing a safe and exciting experience for all of our guests. Our experienced guides will take you on a thrilling journey down the river, where you will experience the beauty of nature and the excitement of whitewater rafting. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have a trip that is perfect for you. Come and join us for an adventure of a lifetime!

History

Wireframe Rafting was founded in 1995 by John and Mary Smith. They had a passion for whitewater rafting and wanted to share that passion with others. They started the business with just one raft and a dream. Over the years, Wireframe Rafting has grown into one of the premier whitewater rafting companies in the country. We now have a fleet of rafts and a team of experienced guides who are dedicated to providing our guests with an unforgettable experience. We are committed to safety and customer satisfaction, and we strive to make every trip down the river a memorable one.

Adventure Awaits You!.

At Wireframe Rafting, we offer a variety of trips for all skill levels.
